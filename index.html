About This Dashboard

Project Overview

The Environmental Light Pollution Dashboard is designed to help schools monitor and manage lighting conditions across their campuses. This system helps identify areas that need maintenance, improves student safety, and supports environmental awareness by reducing unnecessary light pollution.
